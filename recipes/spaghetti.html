Description

Jollof Spaghetti is a delicious and flavorful dish that combines the rich, spicy flavors of West African Jollof rice with the comforting texture of spaghetti pasta. This fusion dish is a popular choice for those looking to enjoy the best of both worlds, offering a unique twist on traditional Jollof rice by substituting rice with spaghetti.

The dish is typically made by cooking spaghetti in a savory tomato-based sauce infused with a blend of spices, vegetables, and sometimes meat or seafood. The result is a vibrant and satisfying meal that is perfect for family gatherings, parties, or any occasion that calls for a hearty and flavorful dish.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato paste
  • Fresh tomatoes (blended)
  • Onions (chopped)
  • Bell peppers (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Ginger (grated)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chicken or beef (optional)
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Cayenne pepper or chili powder (optional for heat)
  • Bay leaves
  • Water or broth
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn - optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Saut√© until the onions are translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the blended fresh tomatoes and tomato paste to the pan. Cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and the oil begins to separate from the tomato mixture.
  4. Add the chopped bell peppers and any mixed vegetables you are using. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. If using chicken or beef, add it now and cook until fully done.
  6. Season the sauce with seasoning cubes, salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper or chili powder, and bay leaves. Stir well to combine.
  7. Add water or broth as needed to achieve your desired sauce consistency.
  8. Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce. Toss thoroughly to coat the spaghetti evenly with the sauce.
  9. Allow the spaghetti to simmer in the sauce for 2-3 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  10. Remove bay leaves, serve hot, and enjoy your Jollof Spaghetti!
